Spaghetti Bolognese is a classic Italian pasta dish made with spaghetti noodles and a rich meat sauce. It's a comforting and flavorful dish enjoyed worldwide.

Fettuccine Alfredo is a creamy pasta dish that starts by cooking fettuccine noodles until al dente. In a separate pan, melt butter and add minced garlic, cooking until fragrant. Reduce heat and pour in heavy cream, stirring constantly until it thickens slightly. Remove from heat and add grated Parmesan cheese, stirring until melted and smooth. Season with salt, pepper, and a pinch of nutmeg. Drain the cooked pasta and toss it in the creamy Alfredo sauce until well-coated. Serve immediately, garnished with additional Parmesan cheese and chopped parsley. It's a simple yet indulgent pasta dish that is loved for its rich and velvety texture.
